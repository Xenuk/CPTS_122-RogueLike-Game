Game Instructions:

Upon starting the game you may choose to Play, Shop, go to Settings, or Exit.
Playing the game will take you into an Intro Menu. It will inform you of gameplay basics.
After this you may click Play again to actually start. You may move via WASD, Up, Left, Right, Down.
You may 'shoot' by using the left mouse button and clicking on the screen. You can change your weapon type.
Changing your weapon type requires selecting 1, 2, or 3 for Pistol, Rifle, and Sniper respectively.
Weapons will use ammo, and can be reloaded by clicking R. 
Your goal as a Player is to survive for 10 minutes to win the game.
After every round you will keep your score, this score will come with you to the Main Menu.
Using Score, you can buy upgrades at the Shop. These upgrades will stay with you in your future games.
You can use Esc on the Keyboard to access the Escape Menu while ingame. 
You can select Exit to exit to the Main Menu.
The Escape Menu you can be exited back to the Game by clicking Play.
